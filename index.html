About Me

Hi, I’m Gitika Padmashali, a passionate graphic designer. I believe that design is more than just aesthetics; it’s about creating meaningful, functional experiences with a lot of patience. I take pride in my ability to blend creativity with precision, ensuring that every project I work on is not only visually appealing but also resonates with the user’s needs.

With a strong commitment to innovation and perfection, I constantly try to push my boundaries and explore new techniques. My work reflects my dedication to continuous learning and improvement. I view each challenge as an opportunity to grow and deliver designs that exceed expectations, bringing ideas to life in unique and impactful ways.

Whether it’s designing logos, websites, or branding materials, I use different softwares like Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign and After Effects. I am also good at photography and making short video edits. I’m always open to collaboration and feedback, as I believe that great design is a result of teamwork and shared perspectives.
